Talent Agency

LITA Agency is a hands-on agency with 20 years of experience representing people of all ages: babies, kids, teens, adults, and seniors across modeling, acting, voiceovers, and extras. .

What makes us different?

We are honest, transparent, and deeply involved at every step. We don’t just “submit and forget.” We guide our talent through the process, direct them on self-tapes, help polish videos, and support them every step of the way to bring out their best.

In addition to talent representation, LITA also offers select public relations services.

We have led PR campaigns for well-known brands and individuals such as Fisher-Price, TOMY International, and Robin Arzón. Whether it’s media visibility, strategic communication, or campaign outreach, we apply the same care and attention to every story we help tell.
